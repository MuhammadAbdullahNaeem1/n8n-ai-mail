# Flowdesk — Billing & Refund Policy

## Invoices
- Invoices are generated on each billing date and available under Settings → Billing → Invoices.
- **Reissuing invoices:** we can reissue any invoice with corrected company details (VAT number, address, company name). Provide the order/invoice reference and the details to add; turnaround is 1 business day.
- EU VAT: reverse-charge applies for valid EU VAT numbers.

## Refunds
- **Duplicate charges:** refunded in full immediately upon verification — no questions asked. Refunds take 5–10 business days to appear depending on the bank.
- **Charges after cancellation:** if a charge occurs after a confirmed cancellation, it is refunded in full. We also confirm card removal in writing on request.
- **General refunds:** monthly plans are refundable within 7 days of the charge; annual plans within 30 days of purchase (pro-rated after that).

## Cancellation
- Self-serve: Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Access continues until the end of the paid period.
- No cancellation fees. Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted. Export your data before that window closes.
- If a customer asks us to cancel by email, we process it within 1 business day and send written confirmation.

## Payment methods
Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and bank transfer / invoicing for annual Team plans of 20+ seats.
