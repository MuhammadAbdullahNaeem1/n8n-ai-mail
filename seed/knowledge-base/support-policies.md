# Flowdesk — Support Playbook

## Response time targets
- Team plan: 4 business hours (priority queue).
- Pro plan: 1 business day.
- Starter/trial: 2 business days.
- Anything flagged **urgent** (outage, data loss, blocked before a deadline, billing dispute): same day, escalate to on-call.

## Common issues

### Login problems / "invalid credentials" after reset
1. Confirm the account email (password resets sent to an alias won't work).
2. Have the user clear cached credentials or try an incognito window.
3. If 2FA is enabled and the device was lost, support can reset 2FA after identity verification.
4. Escalate to engineering if the account shows a `locked` flag — do not ask the user to keep retrying.

### Webhooks not firing
1. Check the account's webhook dispatch log (admin panel → account → events).
2. Known cause: endpoints failing 5+ times in 24h are auto-paused. Re-enable from admin panel.
3. If dispatch shows no events generated, escalate to engineering with the account ID.

### Activity log / audit exports
Compliance exports (up to 24 months) are generated by support within 1 business day. Confirm the requested date range and deliver via secure download link (expires in 72h).

## Escalation
- Engineering escalations: include account ID, timestamps (UTC), and reproduction steps.
- Refund disputes over $500 or legal threats: escalate to management, do not resolve solo.

## Tone
Friendly, direct, no corporate filler. Apologize once when we're at fault, then focus on the fix and a concrete timeline. Never promise features or dates not in the public roadmap.
